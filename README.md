# Yelp Restaurant Market Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes the Yelp dataset to identify business opportunities in the restaurant industry.

Initially, Los Angeles was considered as the target market. However, due to limited data availability, the analysis shifted to Philadelphia, where the dataset provides stronger coverage.

## Objective

To identify opportunities for opening a **mid-priced Asian fusion restaurant** targeting young professionals and students.

## Key Insights

* Philadelphia has over **5,800 restaurants**, indicating a highly competitive market
* The average rating is **3.56**, suggesting room for quality improvement
* Popular categories such as American, Pizza, and Sandwiches are highly saturated
* Some niche categories (e.g., plant-forward dining) show high ratings with lower competition

## Recommendation

We recommend opening a **mid-priced Asian fusion restaurant** that:

* Focuses on high quality and consistency
* Differentiates from saturated categories
* Incorporates modern trends such as vegan-friendly options
